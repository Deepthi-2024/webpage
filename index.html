Environmental Organizations

Conservation Organizations

World Wildlife Fund (WWF)

The World Wildlife Fund is a global organization dedicated to the conservation of nature, wildlife, and their habitats. They work to protect endangered species, promote sustainable practices, and raise awareness about environmental issues.

The Nature Conservancy

The Nature Conservancy is a nonprofit organization focused on conserving lands and waters around the world. They work to protect and restore natural ecosystems, support sustainable practices, and engage local communities in conservation efforts.

Greenpeace

Greenpeace is an international environmental organization that campaigns for global environmental issues, such as climate change, deforestation, and pollution. They advocate for policy changes and engage in direct action to raise awareness and promote environmental solutions.

Environmental Education Organizations

Environmental Defense Fund (EDF)

The Environmental Defense Fund is a nonprofit organization that focuses on solving pressing environmental challenges. They work on issues such as climate change, ocean conservation, and sustainable agriculture, employing science-based solutions and collaboration with businesses and governments.

Earthwatch Institute

The Earthwatch Institute is an international environmental organization that engages volunteers in scientific field research. They offer opportunities for individuals to work alongside scientists on projects related to climate change, wildlife conservation, and ecosystem restoration.

Center for Biological Diversity

The Center for Biological Diversity is a nonprofit organization that advocates for the protection of endangered species and ecosystems. They use legal action, policy advocacy, and public education to combat biodiversity loss and promote conservation efforts.

Climate Change Organizations

350.org

350.org is a global grassroots movement that aims to address the climate crisis. They focus on reducing carbon dioxide levels in the atmosphere to a safe level of 350 parts per million through campaigns, advocacy, and community organizing.

Sierra Club

The Sierra Club is one of the largest environmental organizations in the United States. They work on a wide range of environmental issues, including climate change, clean energy, and public lands conservation. They engage in lobbying, litigation, and grassroots activism to promote environmental protection.

Clean Air Task Force

The Clean Air Task Force is an environmental organization that focuses on reducing air pollution and addressing climate change. They conduct research, advocate for clean energy policies, and work to advance technological solutions for cleaner air and a sustainable future.

Majority of companies planning to recruit this fiscal:

Small and midsize businesses (SMBs) in India are actively planning to recruit full-time professionals for areas like business growth, HR, accounts, and sales for the current financial year, according to a report from apna.co. Its platform has posted over 300,000 SMB jobs from January to May 2023, of which 70% of SMBs have posted job listings for recruitment. Certain cities are looking for specific roles, with Jaipur and Lucknow's high demand for Tele-calling roles, Bhubaneswar and Kochi seeking after business development professionals and Ahmedabad looking for accounts and finance expertise.
